Interesting facts about Camels

  1. Camels ears are furry.
  2. Camels can move easily across the sand because of it's specially designed feet.
  3. When they find water, they drink as much as possible.

Interesting facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are a part of the Iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin colour is an important part of communication among Chameleons.
  • Most Chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap themselves around tree branches.

Interesting facts about Cicadas

  1. A cicada has 5 eyes.
  2. Cicadas have one of the longest life spans.
  3. The cicadas are a superfamily, the Cicadoidea, of insects in the order Hemiptera.

Interesting facts about Panda

  • The giant panda, also known as the panda bear, is a bear native to South Central China.
  • Pandas have great camouflage for thie environment.
  • Giant pandas spend 10-16 hours a day feeding, mainly on bamboo.

Interesting facts about Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting facts about Teddy Bears

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  • The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  • The official term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’.
